Krostuli

Description

Krostuli are aother traditional Istrian recipe usially made for special occasions. This simple yet tasty dish will make your day!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • White wine
  • Butter
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients together
  2. Roll your dough in a thin sheet
  3. Cut stripes
  4. Fold the stripes
  5. Bake
  6. Sprinke powdered sugar
