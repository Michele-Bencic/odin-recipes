Cooked Wine

Description

Now for a drink! The prefered christmas drink in Istria - Cooked Wine!

Ingredients

  • Wine
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Oranges
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add Wine
  2. Add Water
  3. Add Sugar
  4. Add CInamon
  5. Add Orange halves
  6. Mix all the ingredients together
  7. Cook at 80c for about 30 minutes
